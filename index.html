Interested in Aritifical Intelligence?

NextGen AI is not only a place to first learn about AI, but is a place to engage in high-level research (making a unique contribution to a field and writing a paper). Our goal is to educate teens –even those without any AI experience– on the realm of machine learning and give them the guidance to engage in a research project by the end of the program.

Sign Up

Program starts on 4/22 from 1:00 to 2:30 at the Mitchell Park Comunity Center.
Sponsored by City of Palo Alto.

Mission

Our goal is to create a resourceful space where teens can learn about AI and work on projects in Machine Learning. We hope to inspire the next generation through early exposure in AI by providing free GPU power, access to large datasets, as well as mentorship from experts in the field.
