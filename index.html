NextGen AI

Inspiring the Next Generation of AI Researchers.


Mission:   We hope to not only offer a place to first learn about AI, but to offer a place where students can engage in high-level research (making a unique contribution to a field and writing a paper). Our goal is to educate teens –even those without any AI experience– on the realm of machine learning and give them the guidance towards a research project.


Sponsored by City of Palo Alto.

Overview
  With AI being a driving force in technology, AI literacy is becoming increasingly important. AI has proven to be a solution to a wide variety of problems, and will be a tool of the future. We believe that the next generation has the jurisdiction on this technology, and, thus, should be properly educated on it; specifically, we hope to inspire the next generation of ai researchers through a collaborative research environment designed for high schoolers.

  NextGen AI is a research incubator. It is a place where high schoolers can get together every week and work on solving a problem they deem relevant. It is a supportive environment where you will grow in both technical skills and collaborative thinking. By the end of the program, you and your team will have completed a full-length research project that advances the field that you can submit to science fairs or conferences.

  Our program requires no experience in AI, and we welcome everyone to join. After all, a diverse range of thinkers is crucial to use AI for social good; it creates a creative space where innovation is key. We’ll be starting from the fundamentals of math and computer science, and, as long as you keep up with the content, you will have the skills to work on your own AI projects.
