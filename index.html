NextGen AI

Inspiring the Next Generation of AI Researchers.


NextGen AI is a month-long, remote program that will make you an expert in the realm of artificial intelligence and machine learning. Our goal is to create a resourceful space that inspires the next generation through early exposure in AI by providing GPU power, access to large datasets, and mentorship.

Sponsored by Google and City of Palo Alto.

Overview
NextGen AI has been running successfully for over 4 years now!

After completing NextGen, you will not only be comfortable with how ML algorithms work, but you will be on track to finish a full-length machine learning project, which you can submit to science fairs, conferences, or simply add to your portfolio. We have sent numerous high schoolers to top undergraduate AI programs like CMU and Stanford, and have helped college students get internships at Google, Facebook, and Amazon.

Our instructors have years of experience in AI, multiple publications at top journals, and the founders of AI research incubators across America. They have presented work at world-renowned conferences (such as Neural Information Processing Systems) and published textbooks on youth AI literacy.

Instructors

NextGen AI instructors are experts from top AI instiutions such as CMU, Stanford, and Google. They have published books in the field, and have taught for many years.

When and where?

The course is remote, and held over Zoom. Sessions will be 1-2 times per week for the whole month of August, lasting 2 hours each session.

Experience needed?

This course is geared to by introductory, but is still fast-paced. We start with the fundamental math and CS needed, and quickly dive into more complex topics.
