NextGen AI

Inspiring the Next Generation of AI Researchers.


Mission:   We hope to not only a place to first learn about AI, but is a place to engage in high-level research (making a unique contribution to a field and writing a paper). Our goal is to educate teens –even those without any AI experience– on the realm of machine learning and give them the guidance to engage in a research project.


Sponsored by City of Palo Alto.

Overview:


Ultimately, we want to remind you that NextGen AI is not only a place to first learn about AI, but is a place to engage in high-level research (making a unique contribution to a field and writing a paper). Our goal is to educate teens –even those without any AI experience– on the realm of machine learning and give them the guidance to engage in a research project by the end of the program. With extensive mentorship (from Google) and resources (GPU power), we hope that our program will give you the opportunity to submit projects to science fairs or academia level conferences.
